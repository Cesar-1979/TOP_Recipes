Unforgettable Garlic Bread

At first I talked about this dish as a fantastic side for pastas and salads. But who am I kidding, I've eaten a platterful in one afternoon as a snack. What is the secret to making it unforgettable? Use fresh garlic! Do not use garlic powder.

    Ingredients
  • 50 gr unsalted butter (softened)
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • One pinch of salt
  • One loaf of brioche bread
      • Steps
  • Leave the butter out for approximately 30 minutes before starting. This will make it more malleable and will melt into the bread more easily.
  • Chop the parsley very finely.
  • Peel the garlic and mince it. Feel free to use a garlic press.
  • Add the butter, chopped parsley, and minced garlic into a small mixing bowl.
  • Add a pinch of salt, and mix everything together.
  • Slice the bread into thick sticks.
  • Spread the butter on all four sides of each piece of bread.
  • Toast the bread on a large pan on low heat.
  • Flip on each side until golden. Make sure not to let it toast too much. You want it to remain chewy and moist.
  • Serve and enjoy!

    • These breadsticks are addictive. I think some of the most memorable meals I've had had included this garlic bread and our staple Vegan Orzo Shillet or our Vegan Spanish Lentils.
